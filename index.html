Bio

Weezer has been killing it for a long time. Their greatest album of all time is the Blue album.
The blue album, originally released in 1994, continues to be quite popular. So much so, that in 2012,
Weezer performed the entire album from start to finish to a sold out crowd in Orlando, Florida.

While many of the albums released by Weezer over the last twenty five years have featured some great hits,
none of them can compete with the greatness that is the blue album. Of course, there is some debate amongst Weezer fandom
regarding the accuracy of that statement, but those people are wrong.
